SECTION A: INTRODUCTION

About the School-Based ITN Distribution Survey

ABOUT THIS SURVEY

The School-Based Distribution of Insecticide-Treated Nets (ITNs) is a key malaria prevention strategy implemented by the National Malaria Control Programme (NMCP) under the Ministry of Health and Sanitation (MoHS) of Sierra Leone.

This digital survey tool captures real-time data on ITN distribution activities in primary schools across all districts. It collects enrollment data by class and gender, tracks ITN distribution, and automatically calculates coverage statistics.

CLASS-BY-CLASS TRACKING
Record enrollment and ITN distribution for Classes 1-5
GENDER DISAGGREGATION
Track boys and girls separately with automatic totals
AUTO-CALCULATIONS
Real-time totals, proportions, and coverage statistics
BUILT-IN VALIDATION
Ensures ITNs distributed cannot exceed enrollment
